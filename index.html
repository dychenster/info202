Advertising Creative Director. Future Data Scientist. Go big or go home right? After a fun career creating campaigns, developing marketing strategies, and wearing a lot of black, I decided to take the leap and join Berkeley's MIMS '25 cohort to explore how data might be used for equity and justice for all. As a data scientist interested in the intersection of geospatial information and behavioral economics, I'd like to find ways to uproot the current, systemically problematic resource infrastructures and help facilitate the creation of new ones made specifically for a community, designed by that community.

While I've "retired" my creative marketing career, you'll probably still find me wearing black pretty much all day everyday. ☺
